# Our Purpose

Most people focus on predicting sports results.

We focus on helping users understand their own decision-making.

ChezaGame Intelligence Platform enables users to:

- Track betting performance
- Understand betting behaviour
- Improve bankroll management
- Learn from historical decisions
- Receive intelligent recommendations
- Build disciplined betting habits

---

# Problem Statement

Many sports bettors rely on emotions rather than evidence.

Existing betting platforms primarily encourage placing bets but provide limited tools for analyzing long-term performance or improving decision quality.

Users often struggle to answer questions such as:

- Why am I losing money?
- Which leagues am I strongest in?
- Which betting markets perform best?
- Do I chase losses?
- Is my staking strategy sustainable?
- What habits reduce my profitability?

ChezaGame Intelligence Platform addresses these challenges through intelligent analytics.
